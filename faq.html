Text-to-Speech System FAQ

What is the Text to Speech System?
A revolutionary assistive technology that converts written text into spoken words, helping individuals with reading difficulties.
How does it work?
Our system uses advanced algorithms and machine learning to synthesize natural-sounding speech from text inputs.
How do I use the system?
Simply type or paste your text into the input field and click the "Speak" button to hear the audio output.
Can I customize the voice and pitch?
Yes, our system offers a range of voices and pitch options to suit your preferences.
What devices are compatible with the system?
Our system is accessible on desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones with internet connectivity.
How do I troubleshoot technical issues?
Contact our support team at [support@email.com](mailto:support@email.com) for assistance with any technical difficulties.
